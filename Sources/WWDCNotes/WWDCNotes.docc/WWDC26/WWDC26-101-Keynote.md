## Key Takeaways

- Apple introduces Siri AI with powerful new capabilities for a more conversational experience.
- New design improvements and performance enhancements across macOS, iOS, and more.
- Enhanced child safety features with customizable parental controls.
- Integration of Apple Intelligence models for smarter app functionalities.
- Advanced photo editing tools with features like Spatial Reframing and Image Playground.

## WWDC Highlights

@Image(source: "WWDC26-101-ios-27.jpeg", alt: "iOS 27 overview")

- **Event Introduction by Tim Cook**: Focused on the importance of platform advancements and developer community engagement. Celebrated the continuous growth and innovation within the Apple ecosystem.

- **Developer Community Growth**:
  - Over 1,000 app submissions per hour to the App Store.
  - Apple Developer Academies have educated tens of thousands worldwide, fostering the next generation of developers.

## Design and Performance Enhancements

- **Cross-Platform Design Updates**:
  - **Liquid Glass**: Enhanced for improved readability and depth. Now includes a slider for users to adjust clarity from ultra-clear to fully tinted, allowing for personalized app experiences.
  
  @Image(source: "WWDC26-101-liquid-glass-slider.jpeg", alt: "Liquid Glass slider interface")
  
  - **UI Consistency**: New uniform toolbars and expanded sidebars across apps provide better structure and focus, reducing distractions and enhancing usability.

- **Performance Optimizations**:
  - **Faster App Launches**: iPhone and iPad apps launch up to 30% faster by preloading key data, increasing efficiency and user satisfaction.
  - **Photo and Content Loading**: Photo loading is improved by up to 70%, making photo libraries more responsive. AirDrop transfers are 80% faster, streamlining file sharing.
  - **Network Transitions**: More seamless transitions between cellular and Wi-Fi networks, reducing connectivity disruptions and enhancing user experience.
  - **System Animations**: Smoother animations across devices, such as swiping between Home Screen pages on iPhone and transitioning between spaces on Mac.

## App Enhancements

- **Safari**:
  - AI organizes tabs into topics and monitors page updates with the Notify Me feature.
  - Custom extensions can be created easily with natural language descriptions.

- **Photos**:
  - Features like Clean Up, Extend, and Spatial Reframing for advanced photo editing.
  - Image Playground provides creative possibilities through generative models.
  
  @Image(source: "WWDC26-101-photos-features.jpeg", alt: "Photos app features")

- **Shortcuts**:
  - Simplified creation using natural language descriptions, making automation more accessible.

## Developer Tools

- **Apple Intelligence APIs**:
  - Offer new opportunities for developers to integrate AI capabilities into apps using the Foundation Models framework.

- **Xcode Enhancements**:
  - Updated tools and testing environments, including the new Device Hub for unified device simulation.

## Global Rollout

- New features will be available with the latest software releases.
- Siri AI beta will launch later this year, initially unavailable in the EU and China due to privacy and regulatory considerations.
