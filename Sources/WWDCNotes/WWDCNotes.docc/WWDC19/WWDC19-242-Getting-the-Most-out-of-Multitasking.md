# Getting the Most out of Multitasking

Hear lightning talks from experts on the System Experience team to help take your app to the next level. Learn how to structure your application to instantiate your UI, manage resources, and handle when windows come and go. Understand what the lifecycle of a window is, how to target content to a specific window, and how to ensure your content is always up to date.
